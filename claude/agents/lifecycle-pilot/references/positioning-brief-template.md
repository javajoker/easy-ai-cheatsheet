## Brief structure (summary)

A positioning brief covers:

1. **Category** — the market category being entered.
2. **ICP** — ideal customer profile (firmographics, demographics,
   behaviours, problems).
3. **Anti-ICP** — who this is explicitly *not* for.
4. **Value proposition** — one sentence; passes the "so what" test.
5. **Differentiation** — what this does that obvious alternatives
   don't, anchored against named competitors.
6. **Messaging hierarchy** — headline → 3 pillars → proof points.
7. **Voice** — tone, register, what we sound like and don't.
